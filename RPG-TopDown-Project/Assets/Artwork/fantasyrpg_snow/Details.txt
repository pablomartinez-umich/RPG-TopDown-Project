FANTASY RPG - Snow expansion pack (by Franuka) v1.2
-------------------------
Thank you for purchasing this asset pack! This is an expansion to the original Fantasy RPG asset pack
---
Included are all the tiles and spritesheets for each asset, in .png format, transparent background, at 100% and 200% sizes.
You should be able to recreate anything you see on the example images.
The tiles are mostly 16x16px (100%) or 32x32px (200%)
